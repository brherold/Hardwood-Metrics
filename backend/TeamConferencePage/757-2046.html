Mar 19 2022: Change - by Rasinbran on November 15th, 2021

As the new president of the Marietta College Pioneers, I stepped in and saw that the team has reached its lowest point. So, I decided that the team needed some changes. I fired Gary Bishop and hired David Culver in his place. Gary Bishop had served this college great in the past, but The Marietta Pioneers require a coach who is renowned not only for their defence, but also their offence. I spoke with both coaches before making the decision and Gary had some advice for David; "These are fine young men with plenty of potential, if molded correctly, they can shake up the division." Thank you for your time,the Pioneers plan to move up, because we can only go up from here.
