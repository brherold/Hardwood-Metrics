Dec 16 2034: Amazing 2nd-half Turnaround for the Skyhawks - by Rackme32 on October 16th, 2023

Down by a 34-32 count at the half to lowly Colorado School of Mines, The Fort Lewis Skyhawks delighted the home crowd with a 51-13 2nd-half beatdown that featured a 28-0 run and a 10-0 run, quickly turning the game into a runaway 83-47 victory.

Center Isiah Smith scored 16 and yanked down 16 rebounds while Trevor Howard and Steve Jorgenson tied for the scoring lead with 18.


Jan 09 2027: Rico Morris Scores 44 in Skyhawks win - by Rackme32 on July 30th, 2022
