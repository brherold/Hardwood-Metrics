Jan 09 2038: Eagerly Waiting for Home Games - by stlouis40 on April 6th, 2024

The Sioux Falls community and fans have been waiting for hosting some home games. Sioux Falls has had a total of 7 home games through non-conference and conference games thus far. Thats right....SEVEN! The good news is we only have 5 more away games. We are also in 3 way tie in Legends for most games missed with injuries.
