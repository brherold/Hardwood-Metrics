Jan 03 2037: Linfield Shows No Mercy to Mercy - by MahaneyMagic on February 9th, 2024

The Wildcats romped to a 101-61 victory over the hapless Mavericks, with five players scoring in double figures, led by Taylor Christy's 19 points and 11 rebounds.

This win raises Linfield's conference record to 12-1, tied for first place with Albion. That squad happens to be the one that delivered the loss to Linfield.

Nearing the halfway point of the season, the race is really shaping up to be a good one!


Dec 11 2036: Wildcats Win Sixth Straight - by MahaneyMagic on February 1st, 2024

Linfield may be better-known for football (in real life, haven't had a losing season since 1956), but the Sim Wildcats may make a name for themselves this season at the rate they're going.

They stand at 5-0 in league after whipping the Hamline Pipers while having six 'cats get into double figures.

The leading player was Taylor Christy, who scored 22 points and hauled down eight rebounds.

Next up, the Wildcats travel to Arkadelphia, AK to take on the Henderson State Reddies, who stand at 3-2 in conference but only 4-11 overall.
