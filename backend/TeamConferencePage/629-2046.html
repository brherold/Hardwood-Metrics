Press Releases:

Mar 15 2029: Tournament Victory - by OchoCinco on December 10th, 2022

The Magicians have pulled off the upset for their first tournament win since the 2005 season! The tournament berth comes at the heels of their first conference championship in school history and just coming up short in the conference tournament, losing in the championship game. This season also matches the highest win total in school history. No matter where the Magicians finish in the tournament, this season is an excellent building block for next season with only one scholarship player graduating. We look forward to competing in Division 2 next season!


Mar 09 2025: Magical Tournament Run - by OchoCinco on April 30th, 2022

The Magicians have miraculously won the conference tournament and clinched a spot in the Division 3 National Tournament after a losing regular season! This will be the the Magicians' first time in the Big Dance since 2016, and a huge accomplishment for Coach Reno after taking over halfway through the season. We are very proud of our senior leaders for accomplishing this in their final season, and we are excited about the experience this will give all of our returning players to build on!
