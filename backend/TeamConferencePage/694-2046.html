Jan 22 2043: Changes at Berea - by Coach Dawg on January 14th, 2025

January 14, 2025

A new era started at Berea College today. New President, Coach Dawg, was hired to turn the school around after years of unsuccessfully attaining mediocrity. President Dawgs first order of business was to terminate the contract of Felix Munoz and hire Coach Meyer to run the program. The President stated "I would like to thank Felix for all he did for the program. However, with 1 win in the first 17 games this season, there is not much to thank him for. I wish him nothing but the best at his next job, which I assume will be nightshift at 7-11."

Meanwhile, Coach Meyer comes to the program with a long history of successful coaching and player development. "I look forward to making an immediate impact on the recruiting and development side of the program." Coach continued by saying "There are some players here who have some talent - but very few with enough talent to compete for second to last place. We need to change that."

All in all, it will be interesting to see what the two new additions will be able to accomplish in the next couple years.
