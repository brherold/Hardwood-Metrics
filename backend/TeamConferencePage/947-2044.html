Dec 01 2018: 4* Stacey Dugan and 3* Vincent Scroggins both Commit to the Gorloks - by DougDimmadome on April 28th, 2021

Missouri natives Stacey Dugan and Vincent Scroggins both committed to the Webster Gorloks today. Both are slated to be combo forwards in the future. The Gorloks are now looking to fill 3 more openings this season.

They join a top 25 class from last season which include 3-star prospect Larry Richards
4-star prospects Rasheed Cecil and Vincent Harrison as well as 5-star prospect Billy Bob Finley.

"We are going to be a really young team next season, 3 of the players recruited last year are redshirting and we are hoping to add 5 new HS Seniors to the roster" Said Coach Adrian Eastman, "We have a lot of work to do but it is clear that we have a lot of potential heading into the future. The job is not done yet".
