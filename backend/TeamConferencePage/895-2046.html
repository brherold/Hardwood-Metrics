Apr 03 2045: The Eagles fly into Div 1 - by mwatts129 on May 30th, 2025

Thank you to our seniors for a spectacular season. Although the conference neglected to award any of our guys, their hard work does not go unnoticed. This season resulted in our second regular season conference title and conference tournament title in the past three seasons. Each player knew their role and stepped up whenever it was needed. Special shout out to Zach Kline who took on a new role as PG and led the team throughout the season, averaging 7.0 assists. Also, Darren Lemon deserves a shoutout for being sixth man as a senior but being effective in many positions.

Next season will come with a new challenge with our squad moving on to Division 1. Coppola and the boys are ready and will not disappoint.


Mar 26 2043: Couldn't be more proud - by mwatts129 on February 3rd, 2025

With our loss today, our 2043 campaign has come to an end. Although it is not how we wanted it to finish, I could not be more proud of this group of guys. We won our conference tournament, had a 26-game winning streak, and peaked at no. 15 in the "all division RPI ranking". We are excited to take this momentum into Division 2 next year.


Nov 05 2042: We are determined to keep it going. - by mwatts129 on December 23rd, 2024

After a lackluster 2042, we have found ourselves back in Division 3. The guys did not let it bring them down, with a 5-0 start to the season. We are determined to keep this great start going for the rest of nonconference and into conference play.

Want to give a shoutout to Rodger Peters, with 4 game MVPs out of the first 5 games!
