Apr 03 2023: Murdock equals Player of the Year!!! - by CentauryZero on January 13th, 2022

After a largely surprising season, during which, the team I inherited was able to perform at a top 5 conference level, I am anticipating the roll over into the next season and hoping we remain in the same conference. I want to thank Bryce Murdock for his leadership, effort, and development as a player. Due to his steadfast mentality and consistency, he was able to become the conference player of the year for our Wesleyan team! I am originally from Rochester, New York and it’s truly an honor to be able to coach in my home city. Murdock has been integral for my growth as a GM as he’s given me a blueprint on how to develop the players I recruit in the future. I want to thank all the other teams in the conference for working hard and making this years Player of the year vote seem up for grabs from multiple players. Murdock was only considered an honorable mention on the All-Conference team, so it’s unbelievable that he was able to take the award! I’m proud of the team and I’m overly excited for Murdock heading into his Senior season!
