Dec 20 2035: Smoking That Golden Griffin Pack - by JDubs36 on December 11th, 2023

As expected, the Bemidji State Beavers took down their rival, Canisius, in dominant fashion. RS-Fr Lukas Kanas and Sr "Big" Nick Harper used their height advantage to dominate the lowly Golden Griffins. The foes will meet one more time this year, but if history is any indication, the Beavers should continue to roll.


Feb 16 2034: A New Era for the Beavers - by JDubs36 on September 6th, 2023

Following years of inactive presidents and a lack of recruiting, the future for Bemidji State looked very grim. The roster this year has only 3 scholarship players on it. However, new President Joey Boreczky has since helped turn the culture around and thinks are looking up. The hire of Joey Boreczky, along with the hire of new assistant coach Jason Hannon, has the recruiting efforts of the school looking good, with 5 current commits headlined by 3-star Alan Wagner. All of this, combined with the recent Hall of Fame induction of Glen Neal, has people buzzing about the future of Bemidji State.
