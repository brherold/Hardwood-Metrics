Dec 26 2030: Heads roll at Alabama A&M after second 50 point loss. - by Lame Kiffin on March 8th, 2023

Chairman Lame Kiffin has fired Bulldogs head coach Ralph Campbell after a second consecutive 50 point defeat. The Bulldogs have barely given a yelp lately forcing Kiffin to make the move. We need a recruiter. We just haven’t got the players at the moment. Under Campbell’s previous 2 seasons the Bulldogs failed to sign a recruit , taking in 7 walk ons and have not signed a recruit this season.


Dec 12 2030: Bulldogs start off with a win under new leadership - by Lame Kiffin on March 4th, 2023

Alabama A&M hang on for a gutsy 81-78 win over Augusta to notch up the teams first conference win and open with a win under the Kiffin era.
The Bulldogs shot out of the blocks and were up by as much as 10 points early in the game. The more fancied Augusta clawed back control and looked to be too much for the Bulldogs though. But the dogs never gave up and took the lead late and held on for the W. So PF T.Cummings the best with 27 points and 10 boards. Still allot of work to do with only 9 scholarship players on the roster.
