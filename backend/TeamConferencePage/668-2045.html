Apr 04 2044: Lakers end 2044 season with invitational win - by CreekCat11 on April 3rd, 2025

Despite a disappointing 17-24 record, an 11th place VI.14 conference finish, and a first round conference tournament exit, SUNY-Oswego's 2044 season ended on a high note. This week the Lakers claimed an invitational title with a 73-58 win over the St. Thomas Tommies following victories over Mercyhurst, Stonehill, and OK Baptist and eliminating Belhaven in the playoff semis. Senior point guard Rob Milani led the way with 19 points, 6 rebounds, and 4 assists in earning player of the game honors.

The 5-0 run through the invitational games was not enough to save coach Kendall Hyman's job, however, and he was let go as the team seeks a new direction for the coming year and aims to finish in the top half of the conference. New head coach Matthew Manning takes the helm with an incoming class led by 3-star prospect Micheal Hodges and juco transfer Kermit Mueller. They will likely see a lot of early minutes as the Lakers lose six seniors but return forward Ryan Gilmore, who did a little of everything this past season in earning an all-conference selection.
