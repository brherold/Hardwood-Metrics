Oct 05 2017: Nunez's Last Ride - by ArmoredGiraffe on February 15th, 2021

With Coach Nunez having rebuilt this program and secured it's future, he felt that he had accomplished everything he wanted. He officially informed the media today that this year would be his last ride, and there's one way he hopes it ends; with a Natty.

Direct from Marco himself, "I want to win it all. We had a great chance a few years ago and got screwed over by the refs swapping the venues on us and throwing everything out of order. A Final Four appearance is nice, but I will be disappointed if we don't win it all."



Sep 03 2015: Final Four Appearance Caps Historic Two Years - by ArmoredGiraffe on October 23rd, 2020

What a year! Our first Final Four appearance ever for the Rams. Led by a deep group of seniors, the Rams of Framingham State won back to back conference championships, conference playoffs, winning 80 of 95 games the past two years, and capped it all off with a deep tournament run only losing to the eventual champ dkorchak79 and the UC Santa Barbara Gauchos. Congrats to them, they certainly earned it.

A great foundation has been laid. It will certainly be a tough act to follow for our returning players. A huge loss in talent with 6 seniors graduating definitely hurts our chances of competing in Div 1, but some young players will be looking to make a huge impact and will be given the minutes to develop.
