Jan 26 2019: Lynx win 9 in a row! - by Voc on May 14th, 2021

After an underwhelming 5-6 start in conference play, the Lynx have rattled off 9 wins in a row and are now sitting 2 games back in conference V.10.

JR star Jose Luis Aguilera has led the way for the Lynx with a strong 21.1 PPG over the stretch. When asked about his performance coach Rick O`Donnell stated, "He's the leader on this team and has shown why with his recent play."

The question now becomes can Aguilera and the Lynx sneak into the tournament after the rocky start to the season.
