Mar 10 2044: Badgers bow out from the postseason - by CandelabraHarris on March 25th, 2025

With a round 1 exit from the conference playoffs, the 2044 season is winding down in Spring Hill. With this, the players get a small reprieve before turning their focus on the next season. This is also a bittersweet time for the team as a whole, as we get ready to send off fine young men out into world at large.

We'd like to offer our thanks to the following to-be graduates:

Byron Berger, the starting Point Guard leading the charge
Oliver Howe, the Perthite Power Forward and the team's leading scorer
Rico Lea, the starting Center and leading rebounder.


Jan 07 2044: The Spring Hills are alive with the sound of sneakers on hardwood. - by CandelabraHarris on March 8th, 2025

The Badgers, a program ran by the after school tea & crumpets club for the last five seasons, has finally hired a full-time President of Basketball Operations. The prez is pleased to start work with the college board immediately. Though his general appearance, namely clear tan-lines and a faded sticker tattoo saying La Verne has raised some eyebrows.

The program will focus solely on getting a head start into the 2045 and 2046 seasons by putting emphasis on current high school juniors; since the work done by the after school tea & crumpets club, although an admirable cause, has been atrocious.
