Apr 05 2032: Successful season - by Base on June 2nd, 2023

The Warhawks managed to win our conference, both the regular season and the playoffs, then went to the third round in the D3 tournament. This has been my most successful season in charge of the team. Now we're heading back to D2. Losing only one starter I hope my team can develop even further and avoid relegation this time.


Oct 02 2031: A new beginning - by Base on April 8th, 2023

After the unfortunate demotion back to D3 the Warhawks are looking forward to the next season. Every student of Auburn Montgomery sheds a tear for freshly inducted Hall of Famer, Isaac Armstrong and recently fired head coach C.C. Nagle who both contributed much to the team's success in recent seasons. They will be dearly missed. However new HC Stan Marcum is determined to lead the team back to D2 and he hopes that somebody will fill the void left by Armstrong.
