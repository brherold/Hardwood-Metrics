Mar 09 2017: Dragons losing a LOT - by Minnesota Macdoes on February 2nd, 2021

The MSU Moorhead Dragons are looking for a big run in the conference tournament as well in the national tournament. We have 4 starting senoirs who will graduate at the end of the year.

Bojan Zoric: All Conference and Player of the Year in the conference as a junoir. He has the single season record for points scored as well as scoring the most points in his career with 3018. He is avg 25.2 pts and 7.5 assits he is also second in total assits and assists in one season. He also is the all time leader in 3 pointers per game with 3.2 and total with 359. Hall of Famer for sure.


Kevin Randolph: All Conference SF as a junoir. All time leader in Totals Steals with 335. Second All Time in 3 pointers made. And he is 8th All Time in Points with 2209. 15th All Time in Rebounds with 612. 14th All Time in Assits with 215.


Jesus Argueta: All Conference C as a sophmore and junoir. 7th All Time in points with 2271. All Time Leader in Rebounds with 1792. 3rd All Time in Blocks with 228. He is also the All Time leader in Fouls with 555


Danny Barber: All Conference SG as a junoir. 9 All Time in points with 1922. 18th All Time in Rebounds with 503. 3rd All Time in Assists with 619. Second All Time in steals with 307.

We are in for a rough future with only 2 non junoirs or senoirs get into the game. RS FR with 7 games of action with a whopping 2.3 minutes a game even then. And the other FR made only 1 appearence for 5 minutes.

Thank You Senoirs!



Feb 20 2016: Dragons shocked - by Minnesota Macdoes on December 4th, 2020

Today the dragons were shocked 105-104 as our backup SG misses a 3 as time expires. Fresno Pacific has been mediocre to say the least this year. But luckily North Georgia also lost so the dragons stay in a tie for first place with the tiebreaker.


Feb 18 2016: Dragons blow out Nighthawks - by Minnesota Macdoes on December 3rd, 2020

The Dragons absolutley smacked North Georgia, 114-86. The dragons move into first place. Led by Kevin Randolph who earned his second player of the game with 30 points, 5 assists and 3 steals with 5 threes. If the dragons win out they will win VI.5 and earn an automatic bid (I think) into the division 3 national tournament.


Feb 18 2016: Dragons move within one game - by Minnesota Macdoes on December 2nd, 2020

The MSU Moorhead Dragons cruise to a win over Pittsburgh Johnston with Bojan Zoric earning his 6th player of the game award with 30 points and 17 assits. But the big news today came from North Georgia as they got upset big by Concordia Irvine. That sets up potentially a winner take conference tomorrow between MSU and North Georgia.


Feb 11 2016: Dragons sneak out close one - by Minnesota Macdoes on December 1st, 2020

The Dragons escaped an upset by Alabama A&M 89-85 it was a low scoring game for the dragons as they average over 100 points a game. North Georgia stayed 2 games ahead by demolishing Santa Clara 108-81. The dragons will need some help to overtake them. Bojan Zoric captured his 5th player of the game award with 18 points and 16 assits.
