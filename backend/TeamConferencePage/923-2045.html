As the university of Dallas looks to get back up to DI, they look to stability at head coach as they announce they will be keeping the Pittsburgh native Tanner Kerr as there Head Coach of the future. "i am filled with joy as we head into the 2019 season with high expectations. we fell from DI to DIII and we have long ways to go, but i feel like we have the staff and the roster to make a run, y'all better watch out." said Tanner Kerr at his weekly press conference.
