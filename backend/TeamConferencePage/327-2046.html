NC A&T is about winning and since Ive been here I believe I have done just that" Returning every starter from a Historic season has the Aggies dreaming big. Oct 25 2017: Aggies Promoted and say goodbye to best player in school history. - by Cxhawk93 on February 20th, 2021

NC A&T Aggies finish the season with a 25-5 conference record earning 2nd place and a 40-8 overall record, that being the best record in Program history. The man at the helm Coach Braxton is now overall in conference play 119-67 .560 wp and 178-91 .510 wp overall. The most important player on the best was Vance Jensen, he leaves the aggies as the best player literally and statically in program history. Jensen is number one in every scoring category including total points where he scored 3203 points in his career as an Aggie. He ranks top 10 in total steals, assists, rebounds, total field goals(1), free throws(1) and free throw percentage, 3 pointers made(1) and 3 point percentage, Career plus/minus and total plus/minus(1), and career potg awards. He finished with career averages of ppg(18.5) reb(4.9) ast(2.3) stl(1.0) and 3x all conference selections. Jensen also became the first Aggie to be drafted going #29 in the first round to Atlanta. He will be missed but NC A&T in confident that they have players on the roster to earn another promotion to division one. Things are looking up for the Aggies.
