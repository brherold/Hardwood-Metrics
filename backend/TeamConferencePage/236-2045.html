Press Releases:

Mar 10 2044: End of Season Recap - by PBHawks23 on March 25th, 2025

With a heartbreaking loss to Scranton in the conference tourney, the end of the 2044 FIU Panthers season is here. We will be losing two seniors to graduation, Noah Peters and Mateo Mendoza. They greatly helped this team and will be sorely missed.


Mar 05 2044: Panthers Gameday #2 - by PBHawks23 on March 25th, 2025

The Florida International Panthers are the 14th seed in the VI.29 tourney, and their opponent is 3-seeded Scranton University. These two teams have played each other twice, with Scranton coming out on top both times. Coach Walter Bush said “I really want to lan on our starting players, and give them as many minutes as possible to give us the highest chance of winning.”


Mar 05 2044: Drive to the Postseason - by PBHawks23 on March 24th, 2025

The FIU Panthers are in the playoffs, and their opponent is 21-9 Scranton University. Walter Bush, the heads coach, said Wednesday “We have a good and respectable opponent in Scranton, and they have home court advantage.” The coach also acknowledged the recruiting process of Geoffrey Hardy, a JUCO from South Florida. “We are excited to work with him, and we have expressed our interest in his skills.” Sources show that Florida State was also heavily recruiting him, but FIU swooped in and took the #1 spot.


Mar 03 2044: Panthers Gameday #1 - by PBHawks23 on March 24th, 2025

The FIU Panthers will go on the road today to play the Rogers State Hillcats. The Hillcats, who are in 6th in the conference, look to leapfrog Desales University and take 5th place. The Panthers are in desperate need of a win. Coach Walter Bush said, “ I didn’t sugarcoat this game while talking to my players. This is a tough team to beat on the road, and we need to come out hard early.” These two teams played in University Park earlier this year, with RSU coming out on top 76-68.


Mar 03 2044: New Direction - by PBHawks23 on March 24th, 2025

We have decided to focus largely on recruiting and have a prediction of a bad year. Coach Walter Bush said “We anticipate a good conference, with many competitive teams, but we expect to win at least ten games. I have the utmost confidence in our team to deliver in games.” On the heels of a 16 and 27 season, we are focused on rebuilding.
