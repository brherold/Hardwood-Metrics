Nov 12 2033: Stat Madness!! - by Ratsvam12 on August 10th, 2023

Malik 'The Spud' Chatman has done the unbelievable and dropped a triple double in the Kangaroos recent loss to the SW Warriors.
Though it did take double overtime for him to get there, Chatman's 20 points 10 rebounds and 13 assists will go down as the first Triple Double in this programs' history.
Despite the statistical explosion, hardwood universe fantasy basketball managers were still heard complaining about his poor shooting efficiency, and how they lost their FG% & 3PT% category due to his efforts.
