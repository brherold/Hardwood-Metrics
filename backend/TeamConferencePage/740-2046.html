Oct 06 2044: McMurry War Hawks Welcome Toystack as New President to Lead Athletic Vision - by Toystack on April 6th, 2025

Abilene, TX — April 6, 2025 — McMurry University is proud to announce the appointment of Toystack as the new President overseeing the McMurry War Hawks, marking a bold new chapter for the university’s athletic leadership. Toystack will assume immediate responsibility for shaping the strategic direction and performance of the War Hawks, including direct oversight of the university’s acclaimed basketball program.

The War Hawks men’s basketball team soared to new heights last season, finishing fourth in the VI.16 Conference and earning widespread praise for their performance, grit, and team spirit. With Toystack at the helm, the university aims to build on that success, bringing a renewed focus on excellence both on and off the court.

“We’re thrilled to welcome Toystack to the McMurry family,” said University Provost Dr. Linda Whitaker. “His dynamic leadership style and commitment to student-athlete development align perfectly with our mission. We believe Toystack will not only maintain the momentum of last season but elevate our programs to even greater heights.”

Toystack brings with him a forward-thinking approach and a track record of energizing programs through innovation, mentorship, and community engagement. As the new president, he has expressed a clear vision for continued progress.

“I’m honored to join McMurry University at such an exciting time,” said Toystack. “The War Hawks basketball team had an incredible run last season, and I fully intend to keep that momentum going. We’ll focus on culture, consistency, and competition—three things that make champions.”

Toystack’s role will also include strategic planning for athletic facilities, alumni engagement, and supporting academic success for all student-athletes.

As the War Hawks prepare for the upcoming season, fans, students, and alumni alike can look forward to a thrilling future under new leadership.
