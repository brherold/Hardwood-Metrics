Mar 06 2036: Merry Mary Marauders - by LudeBurger on January 2nd, 2024

As the 2036 season comes to a close, the University of Mary are happy to announce the hiring of a new president to take the wheel and steer the program to new seas. Having started at one of the worst programs at the time in 2013 at Michigan Tech and building them into a lower division powerhouse briefly, new president LudeBurger returns to Hardwood for the next great challenge.

"I wanted a team that was in the toughest region to recruit in, the Plains, as well as a team with as little history of success as possible, a place I could truly make my mark. The stars aligned for an opening here in Bismarck."

Although building Mary will be a challenge, LudeBurger is rumored to be close with the president at Dominican University of California and will surely draw inspiration from one of the best programs in Hardwood.
