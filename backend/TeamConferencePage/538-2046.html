Nov 24 2044: Adelphi Above .500 In D2, First Since '18 - by NotSoGood88 on April 20th, 2025

With Adelphi 66-53 triumph over Memphis today, the Panthers (7-4, 1-0 V.2) moved above .500 in Division 2 conference play for the first time in over 25 seasons. Adelphi has not held such a distinction since defeating Tulane, 67-56, on December 8, 2018.

Panthers head coach Pablo Ramirez, then a 29-year old assistant at South Alabama, downplayed the milestone, opting to focus on the ongoing quest for the program's first-ever promotion to Division 1.
