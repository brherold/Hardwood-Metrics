Jan 22 2039: Home Away From Home 01.22.39 - by Logic on June 5th, 2024

The Hartford Informer

Your Hartford Hawks held Berea College scoreless in the last 4 mins and 40 seconds of an eventual 9 point win at the Seabury Center.

Despite playing in Kentucky, Johnnie Ruth looked right at home as he scored 18 on 9 shots and pulled down 13 rebounds(6 offensive).

With this win, Al Lively's team moves to 10-8, 4 games above the relegation zone. The Hawks will need to keep up their performances as they continue their road tour with a game vs Radford.


Jan 20 2039: Strife and Success 01.20.39 - by Logic on June 4th, 2024

The Hartford Informer

After opening up the new year on a high, your Hartford Hawks were brought down to Earth. Two back to back losses by 42 and 13 points.

With just one home game left before their 10-game away trip, Hartford needed to make a statement. While the team disappointed in their previous two matchups, coach Al Lively was confident they could get things back on track.

Offensively, the team still struggled, putting up just 59 points, their 5th consecutive game scoring below 60. But, a win was still secured over Weber State, giving Hawks fans something to look forward to before a grueling away trip.

Dec 30 2038: The Hartford Informer 12.30.38 - by Logic on May 29th, 2024

"We should have won, but we didn't," said Jared Paige, coach of the VCU Rams, in a post-game interview. His VCU Rams(10-11, 4-7 V.8) fell to your Hartford Hawks (9-12, 6-5 V.8) in a shocking upset at Chase Arena.

Your Hawks were without star player Jerry Currie, who leads Hartford in points and steals while holding opponents to 38.9% shooting.

Despite Currie's absence, the Hawks' defense held strong. The Rams shot just 19-55 from the field and 6-22 from 3. On the other end, Bert Hartley scored 15, and Jimmie Cummings put up 14 points with 8 rebounds.

Hawks' coach Al Lively was impressed by his team's performance. The coach stated, "When someone like Jerry [Currie] isn't available you need others to step up, and we saw that we did today."

The Hawks' next challenge is Jim Baker's RHIT Engineers(9-12, 5-6 V.8), in Chase Arena on New Year's Day 2039. Have a Happy New Year's Hawks!
