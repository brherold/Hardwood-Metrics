Feb 11 2044: A Big Come-back Win at Home Against Tampa - by F-22 on March 18th, 2025

A big win happen in Frankfort, KY. In Exum Center, the Thorobreds at a 86-83 win against Tampa Spartans. Being up 45-37 at the half, Kentucky State slowed down the pace in the 2nd half, only scoring 41 points. Tampa outscored State by the score of 46-41. The new "Hot Star" is now Austin Wilder. No. #18 Austin Wilder played for 35 minutes, with 20 points, 7 rebounds, with 5 personal fouls. However, the POTG is Carson Loyd, winning his 4th POTG. He played 40 minutes, 15 points, 13 rebounds, 3 assist, with 4 blocks. Ricky Bartholomew got 2 free throws to win the game with just 10 seconds left.

The next game for the 19-14 (13-10) Kentucky State, is at The College of New Jersey, who is 5-12 at home. Will State win against the Jersey Lions? I guess we will see for the Kentucky State Thorobreds.


Dec 04 2042: Coming Back - by F-22 on January 1st, 2025

It feels good to be at Kentucky State. In my first season at State, we were 21-20. We were doing good. By the second season, I had made friends with the players. We as a team were like a family, in which every team should be. But during the off season, I got fired. But as a basketball manager, I left Kentucky to find more teams. Next thing I know, I'm at Georgetown. I did my best for Georgetown. I met the former Head Coach Mickey Smallwood, who became great friends. But since Smallwood was old, the team was losing its rhythm. Last season, Georgetown went 21-20 (13-17). They went 0-3 in their Invitational, in which they made it to the final, but lost.
By this season, Georgetown got a new head coach, HC Smith, mid-season. Sadly, he is now 0-2 for Georgetown, when they lost to Point Loma, and Arkansas State. As I was looking for next season, I got two new recruits for Georgetown. My very first two recruits. But I felt like I didn't belong there. I then got a text that Kentucky State wanted me back. I said yes. When I came back, I found the recruiting was a mess. I had to take 5 scholarships back because they'll be wasted.
My plane for the rest of this season, and the next is recruiting. Not just winning, but recruiting. I'm going to be recruiting overseas. As we have only one overseas player, Jacob Wright, a Small Forward from Australia. I believe that basketball should be all over the world. I'll be searching in places like China, Japan, and Sweden. During the 2038 season, I made friends with Feng Lee, a redshirt Freshmen. He might've only started 2 games, but he made me love basketball more, because he was a migrant from Beijing, China.
Jersey numbers are imported too. The number I'll be using for is,
-15 (UK's Reed Shepard)
-0 (UK's Otega Oweh)
-1 (UK's Ray Davis, RB in UK Football)
-7 (UK's Andrew Carr)
Thank you. Yes, I did write this down before I said anything. And the ending with this press release is this, God bless America, and Kentucky State!
