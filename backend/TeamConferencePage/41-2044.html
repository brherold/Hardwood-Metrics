Nov 18 2037: Spirits at a all time High - by newuser2183 on March 22nd, 2024

After the most recent win in ot against a really good TCU team, fans morale is at a high. We were led by RS Junior big man Leonard Cowan with a 28 point preformance, without the starting PG. On the same day that happened 4 star Edward Baumgartner committed looking to be the starting PG right away. He is the first 4 star for Florida in years, and is already getting talked about to be one of the best gators of all time. With 2 year Sophomore starter Mario Caputo already established at the PG position, a position battle might ensure. Be on the lookout for Florida to implode at any minute from this controversy.
When reporters asked Leonard Cowan his thoughts he said "I had 28 points without Mario, I dont need him, give me the freshman."
