Mar 02 2045: Thank You Seniors: 2045 - by Twolvesfan21 on May 19th, 2025

Stu Beyer wasn't always hitting his shots, but when he was, it was special. Beyer spent four years in Morgattown being the most confident and energetic player on the floor and for better or worse, kept shooting whether he was 0-8 or 6-9 from deep. Every Mountaineer fan will always have a different favorite Stu Beyer game.
Steward Emerson arrived as a walkon but turned himself into a three year starter who was the definition of consistency. Every game for those 3 years, Emerson was ready to grab a rebound, block a shot at the rim, or dominate in the post. We'll miss Steward Emerson't steady and constant presence at the 5.
